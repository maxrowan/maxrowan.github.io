#weather

This was my senior capstone project, built for Erie Insurance.

Its purpose is to monitor the twittersphere for tweets related to weather. The idea is that if a weather forecast in their area-of-operations is inaccurate, that they'll have as up-to-date information on inclement weather. Since inclement weather is correlated with property damage, this information helps them prepare for incoming claims.
